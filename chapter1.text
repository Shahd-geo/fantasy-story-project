In a quiet village surrounded by ancient trees, a young villager named Sami discovered a strange map hidden beneath the roots of an old oak tree. The map glowed faintly and seemed to whisper when touched. Curious and a little afraid, Sami followed the markings deep into the forest, where the trees grew taller and the air felt alive. Every step he took made the whispers louder, as if the woods were guiding him toward a hidden secret waiting to be uncovered.
