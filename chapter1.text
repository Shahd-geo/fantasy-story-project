In a quiet village surrounded by ancient trees, a young villager named  Elara discovered a strange map hidden beneath the roots of an old oak tree. The map glowed faintly and seemed to whisper when touched. Curious and a little afraid,  Elara followed the markings deep into the forest, where the trees grew taller and the air felt alive. Every step he took made the whispers louder, as if the woods were guiding him toward a hidden secret waiting to be uncovered.

As Elara stepped deeper into the whispering woods, a strange silence surrounded him. The air felt heavier, and the trees seemed to watch his every move. He tightened his grip on the glowing map, feeling both nervous and excited. Each step forward filled him with a mix of fear and determination, as if the forest itself was testing his courage.
