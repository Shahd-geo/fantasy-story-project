Sami is a young villager with dark hair and curious eyes. He is known for his bravery and love of exploring the forest. Although he sometimes feels afraid, his curiosity is stronger. After discovering a mysterious glowing map, Sami decides to follow it, hoping to uncover the hidden secrets of the whispering woods.

Shadow is a loyal wolf with silver-gray fur and sharp blue eyes. He is strong, fast, and always alert. Shadow has a deep bond with Sami and protects him from danger. Though he looks fierce, he is gentle with his companion and follows him faithfully through the mysterious whispering woods.
